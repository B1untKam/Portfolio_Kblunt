VitruMe

YOUR WHOLE BODY. YOUR WHOLE STORY.

A personal health narrative iOS app inspired by Leonardo da Vinci's Vitruvian Man. VitruMe helps people track their complete health story across 10 wellness dimensions — physical, emotional, nutritional, social, intellectual, financial, occupational, spiritual, environmental, and sleep — so they can walk into any doctor's office, life transition, or emergency with their full story in hand.

Built solo using AI-assisted development (React Native / Expo). Currently in private beta testing via Apple Developer and Expo Go. Features include health cards, a daily journal system, emergency access, doctor visit export summaries, and Ruvi — an AI reflection companion.
